About Us

TRUVA (Talent Rating Universal Verification Agency) is Singapore’s first blockchain-powered private agency dedicated to verifying the background, skills, and reputations of foreign workers. Our goal is to protect employers and uplift trustworthy workers through dynamic, data-driven ratings.

Inspired by Jane Lee’s story, we are committed to building a safer and more ethical manpower system — one where trust is earned, documented, and shared.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is TRUVA?

TRUVA stands for Talent Rating Universal Verification Agency. We help employers verify the qualifications, work history, and credibility of foreign workers.

2. Is TRUVA part of the government?

No. TRUVA is a private-sector initiative that complements government frameworks like EP eServices and MySkillsFuture.

3. What does a “verification” mean?

A verification refers to screening a foreign worker — including their degree validation, basic risk score, and work pass status. Full access includes work history, employer reviews, and more.

4. What’s included in “limited search”?

Limited search includes access to basic degree validation and credibility summary. Full search includes filters by job role, country, rating, and red-flag history.

5. How does TRUVA use blockchain?

All verified records are stored on tamper-proof blockchain systems such as Ethereum or Polygon via platforms like OpenCerts or Accredible.

6. How can I integrate TRUVA with my HR system?

We offer full API access and HRMS integration for systems like Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, and BambooHR. Contact us to explore partnership opportunities.
