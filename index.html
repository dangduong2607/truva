About Us

Founded in response to a tragic event, TRUVA is dedicated to building trust in the hiring ecosystem

Our Mission

TRUVA (Talent Rating Universal Verification Agency) is Singapore's first blockchain-powered private agency dedicated to verifying the background, skills, and reputations of foreign workers.

Inspired by Jane Lee's story, we are committed to building a safer and more ethical manpower system where trust is earned, documented, and shared.

Our goal is to protect employers and uplift trustworthy workers through dynamic, data-driven ratings, creating a transparent ecosystem where credibility is the standard, not the exception.

Through our three-layered verification system and blockchain-secured reputation scores, we're transforming how Singapore hires foreign talent.

How It Works

Our three-layered verification system ensures talent credibility and protects employers

Credential Verification

Educational qualifications are directly validated with institutions and stored via blockchain. We verify degrees, certifications, and licenses with tamper-proof security.

Employment History

We verify prior work experience with past employers, identify gaps and inconsistencies, and flag suspicious employment patterns across the ecosystem.

Reputation Rating

Each worker receives a dynamic rating (AAA to C) based on verified background, employer feedback, and periodic re-verification. Updated every 3 years for accuracy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TRUVA

What is TRUVA?

TRUVA stands for Talent Rating Universal Verification Agency. We help employers verify the qualifications, work history, and credibility of foreign workers through a blockchain-powered verification system and dynamic reputation ratings.

Is TRUVA part of the government?

No. TRUVA is a private-sector initiative that complements government frameworks like EP eServices and MySkillsFuture. We work alongside existing systems to provide an additional layer of verification and trust.

What does a "verification" include?

A verification refers to screening a foreign worker including their degree validation, employment history verification, and a comprehensive risk assessment. Each verification results in a dynamic reputation rating (AAA-C) that employers can trust.

How does TRUVA use blockchain technology?

All verified records are stored on tamper-proof blockchain systems such as Ethereum or Polygon via platforms like OpenCerts or Accredible. This ensures that once credentials are verified, they cannot be altered or forged, creating a permanent record of authenticity.

How can I integrate TRUVA with my HR system?

We offer full API access and HRMS integration for systems like Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, and BambooHR. Our team can work with your IT department to implement a seamless integration that embeds TRUVA verification directly into your hiring workflow.

What makes TRUVA different from other verification services?

TRUVA goes beyond basic verification by creating a dynamic reputation score that evolves over time. Our three-layered approach (credentials, employment history, and reputation rating) combined with blockchain security creates a comprehensive trust infrastructure unavailable elsewhere.
